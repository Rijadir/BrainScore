How to Play

Arrange the numbers in order from 1 to N using the swap buttons.

Example:

3 1 2
1 ⇄ 2 2 ⇄ 3

Each button shows which positions it swaps. Click to swap numbers until sorted: 1, 2, 3

  • 20 items with increasing difficulty
  • Each button swaps two random positions
  • 45 seconds per item
  • 1 point for each solved item
  • Click Skip to move to the next item
